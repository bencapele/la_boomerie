About Us

La Boomerie is a crew composed of three friends who used to do private parties in Lille in a spirit of open camaraderie. When we arrived in Paris in April 2019, we decided to formalize the concept to be able to share our vision of the party to a larger number of people and to make the friends play.

Musically speaking, our style is quite varied between electro, house, minimal, breakbeat, and keeping a strong 90's influence. Charmed by the vinyl, we try to keep this specificity in the heart of our programming.
